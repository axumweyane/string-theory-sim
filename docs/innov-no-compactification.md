# Innovation debate 4 — do we even need compactification?

## Heresy
Hiding extra dimensions by compactifying them is unnecessary.

## Evidence (simulations/round_innov-warped.py)
- Volcano potential V(z) = 15/(4(|z|+1)^2) - 3 delta(z), k=1: shooting from the
  delta-matching condition reproduces the zero mode (z+1)^{-3/2} to **4e-5** over 60 units;
  normalizable (integral exactly 0.5) — a 4D graviton with an INFINITE fifth dimension
- **0** negative-energy bound states (zero mode is the threshold ground state)
- Gapless KK continuum; its correction to the brane potential falls with fitted power
  **-2.03** (theory: -2) — 4D Newton dominates at long distance
