# Innovation debate 2 — is dimension emergent?

## Heresy
Spacetime dimension is a scale-dependent observable, not fundamental input.

## Evidence (simulations/round_innov-emergent.py)
- Spectral dimension d_s(sigma) from exact heat kernels (Fourier), MC-validated (1.6%)
- Control (isotropic 4-torus, L=512): plateau **4.03 ± 0.20**
- Engineered geometry (2 fast + 2 slow directions, r=0.005): runs **2.29 (UV) -> 4.02 (IR)**,
  monotone rise, transient +0.44 lattice overshoot (present in control too, reported not hidden)
- One validator catch: first run's finite-size collision (L=64) faked d_s ~ 2.7 in the IR —
  scale separation fixed before any conclusion was drawn
- Plot: simulations/outputs/innov_emergent_dimension.png

## Frontier map
1. **Why the heresy survives as a concept:** dimension measured by diffusion demonstrably runs
   with scale. But our toy's "4" was smuggled in by construction — it demonstrates the
   observable, not an emergence mechanism.
2. **What protects string theory:** its D comes from worldsheet anomaly cancellation
   (debate 1), a different logic that no computation here connects to diffusion dimension.
3. **Frontier:** whether string theory's own UV exhibits dimensional reduction. The
   running-d_s phenomenon itself is KNOWN — CDT's headline result (Ambjorn-Jurkiewicz-Loll,
   PRL 95 (2005) 171301; [CDT review, arXiv:2401.09399](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.09399)).

Mathematical result only; experimental validation required for any claim about reality.
