# Novelty check: already known

## Hypothesis
The massless-state count jumps discontinuously at the self-dual radius R=1 (enhanced symmetry point)

## Verdict
Already in the literature.

Enhanced gauge symmetry at the self-dual radius R = sqrt(alpha') is a textbook result of bosonic string compactification: extra massless vectors at R=1 assemble into SU(2) x SU(2). Covered in Polchinski Vol. 1 ch. 8 and reviews of T-duality (Giveon-Porrati-Rabinovici).

## Citations
- [Polchinski, String Theory Vol. 1, ch. 8](https://doi.org/10.1017/CBO9780511816079)
- [Giveon, Porrati, Rabinovici — Target space duality in string theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9401139)

## Reasoning
Direct textbook match: enhanced gauge symmetry at the self-dual radius.
